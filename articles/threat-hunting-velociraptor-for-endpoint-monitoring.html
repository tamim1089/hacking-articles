Introduction to Velociraptor

Velociraptor is a free and open-source software project developed by the Velocidex Company. It is generally based on GRR, OSQuery, and Google‚Äôs Rekall tools. Velociraptor allows users to collect Forensics Evidence, Threat Hunting, Monitoring artifacts, Executing remote triage process.
